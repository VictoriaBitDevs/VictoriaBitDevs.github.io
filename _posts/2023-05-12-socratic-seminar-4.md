## Announcements
Please join us for our fourth socratic seminar in the city of Victoria. Please make sure you are RSVP'd, an identifier (but not a full name) is required for entry.

As a reminder, the ground rules of BitDevs are as follows:
1. No photos, videos, or recordings
2. [Chatham House Rule](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chatham_House_Rule): you may reiterate the contents of the meeting without attribution

These rules exist so that BitDevs participants can speak freely within the event
